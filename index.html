About VEM 2025

The 13th Workshop on Software Visualization, Maintenance and Evolution (VEM 2025) aims at fostering the integration of the software visualization, evolution and maintenance communities, providing a forum where researchers and practitioners from academia and industry can present their work and exchange ideas on the principles, practices and innovations related to their respective areas of interest. VEM is also a great opportunity for strengthening research group collaborations, as well as identifying new research opportunities.

In 2025, VEM is co-located with the 16th Brazilian Conference on Software: Theory and Practice (CBSoft 2025).
